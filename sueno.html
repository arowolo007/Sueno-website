About Sueno FC

Founded in 2010, Sueno Football Academy is Abuja's premier football development center, dedicated to nurturing young talents and preparing them for professional careers both in Nigeria and internationally.

State League Champions

Competing at the highest level in Abuja's state league, our academy team has won multiple championships and continues to dominate the competition.

International Exposure

We've produced players who have gone on to play in Europe, Asia, and other African nations, with several representing Nigeria at youth levels.

Holistic Development

Beyond football skills, we focus on character building, education, and life skills to prepare our players for success on and off the pitch.

Our Philosophy

At Sueno FC, we believe in developing technically proficient, tactically aware, and mentally strong footballers. Our training methodology combines modern European techniques with the natural flair and creativity of African football.

Located in the heart of Abuja, our state-of-the-art facilities include two full-size pitches, a gymnasium, and classroom spaces for academic support.

Meet some of our standout players who have represented Sueno FC and gone on to achieve greater heights in their football careers.

Victor Osimhen

Napoli, Nigeria National Team

Position: Striker Age: 24

Former Sueno FC youth player who went on to win the Serie A Golden Boot and become one of Africa's most valuable players.

Samuel Chukwueze

AC Milan, Nigeria National Team

Position: Winger Age: 23

Developed at Sueno FC before moving to Villarreal, now starring for AC Milan in Serie A.

Wilfred Ndidi

Leicester City, Nigeria National Team

Position: Midfielder Age: 26

Sueno FC graduate who became one of the Premier League's top defensive midfielders.

Kelechi Iheanacho

Leicester City, Nigeria National Team

Position: Forward Age: 26

Started his journey at Sueno FC before moving to Manchester City's academy.
